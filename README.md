# ⚡ EcoTracks – Smart Energy Monitoring & Energy Saving Dashboard

EcoTracks is a smart web-based dashboard developed during **HACKNEX 2025**, the Internal Hackathon organized by the **COGNEX Club** at **KVG College of Engineering** on **24 October 2025**.

The project aims to help industries, commercial buildings, and other organizations monitor energy consumption, analyze usage patterns, identify inefficient power consumption, and receive intelligent recommendations to reduce energy wastage and improve energy efficiency.

## 🚀 Features

- 📊 Real-time energy monitoring dashboard
- ⚡ Live power consumption visualization
- 📈 Daily, weekly, and monthly analytics
- 💡 Smart energy-saving recommendations
- 🌱 Energy efficiency score calculation
- 🔋 Energy savings tracking
- 🔌 Device-wise energy usage simulation
- 📉 Interactive charts and analytics using Chart.js
- 🌙 Light and Dark mode support
- 👤 User profile customization
- 📱 Responsive and modern user interface

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of EcoTracks is to provide an intelligent platform for monitoring energy usage and promoting efficient energy management. The dashboard helps users visualize electricity consumption, identify areas of excessive power usage, and implement practical energy-saving strategies to reduce operational costs and support sustainability.
